About the Platform

E-Prison Access is a modern, web-based system designed to replace outdated manual processes with a secure, digital-first approach. Our platform digitizes prisoner records and automates the entire visitor lifecycleâ€”from registration and ID verification to visit scheduling and entry control.

Developed using the robust Django framework, this system enhances security and operational efficiency, aligning with the goals of smart governance and digital transformation for the Kerala Prisons & Correctional Services department.

Key Features

A suite of tools designed for security and convenience.

QR Code Entry

Secure, touchless entry for approved visitors using unique QR codes, minimizing wait times and manual checks.

ID Verification

Admins can digitally verify uploaded photo IDs, ensuring every visitor is properly identified before their first visit.

Emergency Visits

A special, prioritized queue for family members to request urgent visits, subject to security protocols.
