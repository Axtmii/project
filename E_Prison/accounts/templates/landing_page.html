About the Platform

E-Prison Access is a modern, web-based system designed to replace outdated manual processes with a secure, digital-first approach.

Our platform digitizes prisoner records and automates the entire visitor lifecycleâ€”from registration and ID verification to visit scheduling and entry control. Built with cutting-edge security measures and user-friendly interfaces.

Developed using the robust Django framework, this system enhances security and operational efficiency, aligning with the goals of smart governance and digital transformation for the Kerala Prisons & Correctional Services department.

Key Features

A comprehensive suite of tools designed for security and convenience.

QR Code Entry

Secure, touchless entry for approved visitors using unique QR codes, minimizing wait times and manual checks while ensuring maximum security.

ID Verification

Advanced digital verification system for uploaded photo IDs, ensuring every visitor is properly identified and authenticated before their first visit.

Emergency Visits

Special prioritized queue for family members to request urgent visits, with streamlined approval process subject to security protocols.

Enhanced Security

Multi-layer security protocols with real-time monitoring, ensuring the safety of all visitors, staff, and inmates at all times.

Real-time Tracking

Live tracking of visitor status, from entry to exit, providing complete transparency and accountability throughout the visit process.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices, allowing users to manage visits from anywhere, anytime.
